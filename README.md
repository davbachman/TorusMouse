# Torus Mouse

Play online: [https://davbachman.github.io/TorusMouse/](https://davbachman.github.io/TorusMouse/)

Torus Mouse is a split-screen browser maze game. The left panel shows a 3D torus overview with the mouse and cheese decals sliding across its surface as the toroidal maze re-centers around the player. The right panel shows the mouse's first-person view inside the maze, where you collect all 10 cheeses before advancing to the next level.

## How to Play

- `Left Arrow`: turn left
- `Right Arrow`: turn right
- `Up Arrow`: move forward
- `Space`: start the game and advance after clearing a level
- `F`: toggle fullscreen

## Goal

Find all 10 cheeses in each maze. The cheese counter in the upper-right corner tracks your progress, and the maze grows as you clear levels until it reaches `16x16`.
